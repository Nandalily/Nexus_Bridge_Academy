Academic Programs

Our Programs

Structured, NCDC-aligned programs for both Ordinary and Advanced Level learners in Science and Computing.

O–L
Ordinary Level
Senior 1 to Senior 4

Ordinary Level Science & Computing

The Ordinary Level program serves S1 through S4 learners and is designed to build strong foundational knowledge across all five Science and Computing disciplines. All modules are aligned with the Uganda National Curriculum Development Centre (NCDC) O-Level syllabus and directly prepare learners for the Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) national examinations.

Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE)

A–L

Senior 5 to Senior 6

Advanced Level Science & Computing

The Advanced Level program is designed for S5 and S6 learners pursuing higher academic achievement in Science and Computing. Content is deeply aligned with the NCDC A-Level syllabus and prepares students for the Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education (UACE). Modules are structured to support rigorous study and conceptual mastery at a university-preparatory level.

How We Teach

Learning Delivery Model

A
Self-Paced Learning

Students access structured learning modules and study at their own pace. Lessons are available on-demand — no fixed timetable required.
